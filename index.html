5.1 Disability Resources

How to Use This Page

This page is designed to help you quickly find contact information and access points for disability benefit programs across Canada.

At the top of the page, you will see expandable sections labeled Federal Programs and each Province or Territory. These sections organize programs by region so you can easily locate the services that apply to you.

To use the page, simply click on the heading for the region you want. For example, click Federal Programs to view nationwide disability programs, or click your province (such as Alberta, Ontario, etc.) to see programs available in that specific region.

When you click a heading, it will expand and display detailed information, which may include:

  • Program names
  • Direct website links
  • Phone numbers
  • Email or contact form information
  • A short description of what the program provides

Review the listed programs and use the provided contact details or links to reach the appropriate government department or service. These contacts will guide you on eligibility, applications, and accessing disability benefits available to you.

You may open and close different regions at any time to compare programs or find additional supports.
